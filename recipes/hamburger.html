Ingredient List

  • Lean ground beef
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Dried Parsley
  • Salt and pepper
  • Crumbled feta cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat outdoor grill
  2. Knead together the ground beef, parsley, salt, and pepper in a bowl
  3. Form the mixture into 8 equal-sized balls, flatten to make thin patties
  4. Place about 1/4 cup of feta cheese onto each of four of the patties
  5. Top each of the patties with cheese, with one of the patties without
  6. Press the edges together to seal the cheese into the center
  7. Cook the burgers on preheated grill
