Ingredient List

  • Italian Sausage
  • Lean ground beef
  • Minced Onion
  • Cloves garlic, crushed
  • Can of crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Canned Tomato Sauce

Steps

  1. Cook meats
  2. Cook onion, garlic over medium heat
  3. Stir in tomatoes, paste, and sauce, water
  4. Bring a large pot of slightly salted water to a boil
  5. Preheat over to 375 degrees F
  6. Spread meat sauce, then arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over sauce
  7. Bake in preheated over for 25 minutes
