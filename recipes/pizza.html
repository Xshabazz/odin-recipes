Ingredient List

  • Refrigerated pizza dough crust
  • hummus spread
  • Sliced Bell Peppers
  • Broccoli Florets
  • Shredded Monterey Jack Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat over to 475 degrees F
  2. Roll out pizza crust on a pizza pan or baking sheet
  3. Spread thin layer of hummus over the crust
  4. Arrange sliced peppers and broccoli over the hummus
  5. Top with shredded cheese
  6. Bake in the preheated over until the crust is golden brown
  7. Slice and serve
